Some background

I have been born and living now in Vietnam. I like my country because a number of good friends I have here.

Most of my time is dedicated to my love: Viet Phuong. I spend most of time with her. She and I like travelling alot. So we plan to travel around my country every year.

Since the childish years I am big fan of software and all things related. I had run my first programs on a scientific calculator and since then all my life is about developing software.

Of course to make good work a man needs good rest. I love cycling on my bike everywhere, making photos, looking at people's life.

How I do my computing

I use a Dell Vostro at work and a MacBook Pro at home. My wife has a Vaio PC and sometimes I use it. In my opinion, I prefer MacBook Pro because its OS (Mac OS X) has a good interface with a high performance so that I can do almost what I want to. And most of my time, I used MacBook Pro.

Before that, I used a Vaio PC for my study at the university. It works OK. I installed Ubuntu 9.10 on it. And I feel Linux is very good. I have tried some distros and found that they are easy to use. But recently, Ubuntu changes to Unity interface and I don't like that. I will change to another distro.

My company provide my an external monitor. And I found that it is very useful for programming. With a large monitor, I can see more information in one screen.

I use Windows on Vaio because it came with the laptop, with lots of helpful tools made by Sony so that I can connect to wireless networks and play DVDs.

I do all my work in Atom editor. This is new and good editor. Sometimes I use Emacs for small jobs. But Atom allow me to view all files and folders inside a project and that's very helpful for me.

I use email mainly for registering with Internet sites I go on. I use Yahoo! Mail for this. I also use Gmail to connect to my friends, especially for contacting my wife. I use Thunderbird for getting from company mail server because it doesn't have a Web mail app. I prefer to use Web mail because I don't want to install too much things to my PC.

I never download attachments from people I don't know. If I know the person and they send me an attachment then I'll download it and open it as I love the funny pictures I get sent in zip files. I sometimes get emails from friends warning of scams where people pretend their car has broken down or something. I forward these emails to all my friends so they also know the dangers.

I get a lot of spam emails and I don't understand why. I'm not stupid so I don't send money to people in Nigeria. I like the emails I get about competitions though, and I normally enter these as I'd quite like to win a new MacBook.

I use Social Networks. I use Facebook, Twitter to follow celebrities I like. On Facebook, I only make friend with my real friends. My wife and most of my friends use Facebook so that is the best way to keep in touch with them. I use Twitter to connect to my Japanese friends because they use Twitter more than Facebook. Besides, Twitter to a good way to get updated news all around the world. Facebook has too much problem with the advertisements and most contents it shows me is not interested to me. Therefore, I don't want to spend time on it.

When I'm on the Internet I'm always logged into Facebook and Twitter. Also it means I don't have to type in my password all the time. My password is easy to remember as it's my cat's name. A friend told me this was unsafe, but I don't see how anyone could possibly guess it.

Sometimes Facebook is not so good, especially when you get people bullying you on there who don't agree with you. I hate bullies so I always put them in their place, and my messages are always in capitals so they know how wrong they are.

I'm quite careful with my computer. I make sure I'm safe by having anti-virus. I do find anti-virus annoying because they keep telling me to update, but I just close the messages as I think this is a scam to make me buy newer versions that don't do anything special.

I'm very careful with my files. I backup my important files and folder to cloud. I use github and bitbucket to store my source code. I have somes privates project and I don't want to publish them so I choose bitbucket for them. For other works, I use github.
